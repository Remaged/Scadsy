Welcome to SCADSY

SCADSY is an open source school administration system. SCADSY helps schools manage their student information in an organized way. SCADSY also provides a rich framework for module development that is based on CodeIgniter. Feel free to help and expand the project!

Who is SCADSY For?

SCADSY is right for you if:

  • You want a school administration system with a small footprint.
  • You need exceptional performance.
  • You need broad compatibility with standard hosting accounts that run a variety of PHP versions and configurations.
  • You want a school administration system that requires nearly zero configuration.
